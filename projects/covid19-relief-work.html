Covid-19 Relief Work

What is Covid-19 Relief Work ?

With the help of 25 partners, the project successfully provided various forms of SOS aid to a total of 20K beneficiaries from the sex workers community in G.B. Road, Sanjay Camp Chankayapuri, and Pulbangash community in New Delhi. These included essential supplies such as ration, sanitary napkins, hygiene kits, and milk powder packets, as well as financial aid, phone recharge, stipends to community leaders, transport, and phone handsets to students.

Project SEL- COVID Kit:

An aim to enable children with tools to develop social-emotional coping skills at home to regulate themselves emotionally and be able to manage the crisis at hand on a day to day basis. The kit offered 6 day engagement in a week, self-paced activities contextualized to the pandemic crises and promoted parent-child participation.

Project Mission Bridge:

An initiative aimed to bridge the gap between state welfare schemes and beneficiaries, focusing on 100 families from urban slums. By addressing structural, mental, and accessibility barriers, the project helped these families avail the rightful safety nets offered by the government.Â
