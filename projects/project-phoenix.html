Project Phoenix

Holistic Community Mental Health Solution.

What is Project Phoenix ?

Project Phoenix was created with a vision to create a more just and equitable society, where marginalized youth from sex workers community , child care institutions and urban slums have access to resources to break the cycle of trauma, reach their full potential and live a life of dignity and purpose.

A 1-year intervention, Project Phoenix
offers Community Mental Health Care
Solutions focusing on

  • Developing 21st-century life skills through transformative SEL
  • Trauma healing through trauma informed therapy
  • Awareness on citizens and human rights through safety sessions.
  • Building skills with caregivers through sensitization workshops.

We adopt a 360˚ intervention model with a human rights and social justice lens that comprehensively addresses the problem of marginalized youth not having a dignified life owing to structural inequalities and intergenerational trauma.

Testimonials

“Project Phoenix transformed my life. I used to be acutely self-conscious about my mother being in the sex trade. It was here that I learned to understand, even accept, my circumstances and move beyond them. It made me realize the lengths to which my mother had to go, just to raise my brother and me. Slowly, my anger at her being a sex worker was replaced by respect, and this gave me a lot of mental peace.
