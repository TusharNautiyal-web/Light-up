Rishta Khud Se

Systematic integration of SEL (School Mental Health Solution)

What is Rishta Khud Se ?

Rishta Khud Se is a comprehensive SEL intervention in a form of continued support through Social and Emotional Learning workshops with children, capacity building sessions for teachers and awareness sessions with parents that aims to bring a positive shift in their emotional well-being and mental health.

  • ED-volution: Teacher Training Programme, a continued support through in-depth training, feedback sessions, group discussions on pedagogies, incorporation of SEL frameworks in classrooms, and adapting SEL teaching methodology.
  • Building Connection: Student SEL Workshops - We train teachers to deliver SEL workshops to enable children to develop 21st-century SEL skills, healthy coping mechanisms and provide a safe space for expression and exploration of self
  • Breaking Barriers: Parent Sensitization Programme is aimed at equipping them with better parenting techniques, increased participation, and providing a space for parents to de-stress that will advance the comprehensive development of a child.

Each school is given focus for 3 years, where SEL frameworks are set in the school eco-system to aid educators in delivering better pedagogical practices and parent sensitization programs are conducted to build healthier home environments. In the due process of training teachers and equipping parents, the students are empowered with 21st-century SEL skills.
