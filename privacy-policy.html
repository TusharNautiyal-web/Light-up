Privacy Policy

Light Up (Emotions Matter foundation) is fully aware of the care to be taken about the information of our supporters, (also includes volunteers, beneficiaries, and donors). We appreciate your trust and we are committed to act with utmost care and due diligence. By visiting the website, you are accepting the practices defined in our policy.

The information collected from our supporters helps us to have personal relationships and improve your online experience. Any information you give us is stored with utmost care and security. This information helps us in serving you better. Needless to say, we are bound to cooperate fully when the situation requires us to do so, like by law or legal process, we have to provide information about our supporters.

Light Up (Emotions Matter foundation) will not share any of your personal details with any third party without prior approval from you. However, Light Up (Emotions Matter foundation) does have the liberty to share the personal information with our internal team to better serve you and the needs of our organization. Once you register with Light Up (Emotions Matter foundation)/subscribe for our newsletter, you authorize Light Up (Emotions Matter foundation) to contact you via e-mails, text messages, phone calls and other means.

Use of cookies: Cookies are a small amount of data, mostly alphanumeric identifiers that are transferred to your computer's hard drive. They are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser. This helps our system to recognise your browser the next time you enter our site and helps us serve you in a better and faster way.

Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. To take advantage of the website's unique features, we recommend that you allow cookies.

Governing law and jurisdiction: This policy is governed by the laws of India. Dispute if any, relating to any matter relating to or arising out of this policy, shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the appropriate courts at New Delhi.
